Math Studying Guide

Finals will soon be upon us and unfortunately the math final is comprehensive. Rather than rush to study for all of your finals at the end of the year, this website has all of the formulas that you might need to know.

Formulas

Trigonometry
  • Sinθ = y/r , Cosθ = x/r , Tanθ = y/x
  • Sine Law: b/SinB = a/SinA
  • Cosine Law: c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2abCosC
Quadratics
  • Vertex Form: y = a(x-p)^2 +q
  • Standard Form: ax^2 + bx - c
  • Quadratic Equation: x = (-b +-((b^2)-4ac)^0.5)/2a
Radicals
  • (ma^0.5)(nb^0.5)= mn(ab)^0.5
